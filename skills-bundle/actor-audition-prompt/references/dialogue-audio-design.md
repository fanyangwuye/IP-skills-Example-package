# Dialogue / Audio Design / 台词与声音设计

Read this when filling the Dialogue/Audio/Subtitle Plan and the per-segment audio notes. Purpose: make speech, pauses, and ambient sound directable and Seedance-executable, not just "有台词".

> Rule: sound is performance. A pause or a breath often carries more than the line.

---

## 1. 台词节奏 (Speech Rhythm)

- Mark speed per line: 快 / 中 / 慢 / 渐快 / 渐慢.
- Short clipped lines = tension. Long flowing lines = control or rambling.
- Speed change mid-line signals emotional shift.

## 2. 停顿与气口 (Pauses & Breath Points)

- Mark `[停顿0.5s]` `[停顿1s]` `[长停顿]` at decision points and before key words.
- 气口 = where the actor breathes. Place before an emotional reveal: `[吸气][停顿]我没事。`
- Silence before a line builds weight; silence after lands impact.

## 3. 重音 (Emphasis)

- Underline or mark the stressed word: "我**从来**没说过那句话。"
- Move emphasis to change meaning — test which word carries the subtext.

## 4. 字幕决策 (Subtitle Decision)

- Decide per segment: 需要字幕 / 不需要字幕 / 仅关键句字幕.
- Subtitle rhythm: one line at a time, appear on speech, not before.
- For silent emotional beats: no subtitle, let the face carry it.

## 5. 旁白 / 画外音 (Voiceover / Off-screen Voice)

- 旁白 (narration): internal monologue or storytelling layer; mark tone (冷静/沙哑/回忆感).
- 画外音 (off-screen): another character or director speaking from outside frame.
- 导演画外音 (director off-screen note): for audition scenes, e.g. "再来一条，这次收一点" — drives the actor's adjust beat.

## 6. 环境声 (Ambient / Room Tone)

- Always specify base room tone: 安静室内 / 空调嗡声 / 雨声 / 街市嘈杂.
- Functional sounds as beats: page turn, pen tap, footsteps approaching, door, chair scrape, phone buzz.
- Use a sudden sound (door slam, phone) as an emotional trigger.

## 7. 沉默时长 (Silence Duration)

- Treat silence as a timed beat: `[沉默2s，只有呼吸声]`.
- Long silence + held face = pressure. Use sparingly for max impact.

---

## Seedance Audio Wording Examples (copyable)

- `台词："你到底想怎样。" 语速慢，句末下沉；说"到底"时重音；说完[停顿1s]再移开视线。`
- `无台词；环境声为安静室内+轻微空调嗡鸣；角色一次深吸气后缓慢呼出，喉结滚动。`
- `画外导演声："停，再来一次，眼睛别躲。" 角色听到后[停顿0.5s]，重新抬眼，呼吸收住。`
- `旁白（冷静、略沙哑）："那是我最后一次见他。" 同时画面为角色背影静止。`
- `背景：雨声渐强；角色台词被一声闷雷打断，本能停顿，眼神闪一下再继续。`

## Common Mistakes

- 台词塞满每一秒，没有呼吸空间 → 显廉价、不可信。
- 全程同一语速 → 没有情绪曲线。
- 字幕先于台词出现 → 出戏。
- 忽略环境声 → 画面"哑"，缺乏临场感。
